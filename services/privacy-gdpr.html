Privacy & GDPR

Problem we solve

Many organisations collect personal data without clear oversight, creating compliance gaps and exposure to fines.

Our approach

We map data flows, assess risks and implement governance so privacy obligations are met across the board.

Typical deliverables

  • Data processing inventories
  • Data protection impact assessments
  • Privacy policies and consent language
  • Staff training and awareness sessions

Outcomes

  • Reduced breach and penalty risk
  • Demonstrated regulatory compliance
  • Greater trust from customers and partners
