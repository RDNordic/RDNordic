Privacy Notice

We do not use cookies or analytics and we do not embed third-party tracking scripts. The only personal data we receive is what you choose to send us by email.

Who we are

R&D Nordic. Email: contact@rdnordic.com.

Data we process

  • Messages you send us (your email address, name if provided, message and any attachments) so we can reply and follow up.

Hosting

The website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub, as the host, may log visitor IP addresses for security and abuse-prevention purposes. We do not access or use those logs. See the GitHub Privacy Statement for details.

Email

We use Proton Mail as our email provider. Messages in our mailbox are stored with zero-access encryption.

Lawful basis

GDPR Art. 6(1)(b) (to take steps at your request before a contract) and Art. 6(1)(f) (our legitimate interests in responding to enquiries and maintaining records).

Retention

We keep enquiries for up to 12 months, then delete them unless legal obligations require longer. You can ask us to delete earlier.

Your rights

You can request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, or object to processing, and you may complain to Datatilsynet (the Norwegian Data Protection Authority).
