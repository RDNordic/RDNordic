About Me

With 13 years of experience in R&D in the public sector, I started my career in healthcare, working directly with patients.

Over time, my interest in research grew, and 7 years ago, I transitioned into the research field, publishing several papers.

Currently, I work as a Data Privacy Advisor and Research & Development Advisor at the University of Inland Norway.

I also serve as an AI Ambassador, training academic and administrative staff on integrating AI into their work.

As a member of KI-faggruppe at Digi Innlandet, I contribute to AI strategy discussions in the region.

My expertise includes project management, grant funding preparation, GDPR compliance, and research support.
